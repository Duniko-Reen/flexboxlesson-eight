Hi. I'm Duniko.

I help organizations optimize their social impact.

I am an MBA student who thrives on helping organizations optimize the reach, impact, sustainability, and profitability of their social outreach.

When I am not working, pursuing side projects, or listening to my favorite podcasts and audiobooks, I can be found hiking or looking for community asset-building opportunities

Strategy

I connect, capacitate, and guide organizations in collaborating with key stakeholders to create sustainable community-wide development initiatives. To this end, since 2014, I have been instrumental in helping two local (South-East Asia) organizations raise over US$3.3million with direct impact on over 20,000 children and their families.

Technology

I see technology as a key driver not only in identifying social needs, but also in helping stakeholders craft and monitor sustainable solutions for scaleable impact. Central to optimizing such application is bridging the divide between organizational information systems and technology experts on the one hand, and operations and policy experts on the other. In this sense, I see myself as a bridge-builder, helping organizations build bridges internally and externally to and between their stakeholders.

Inspiration

I was born and raised in East Africa to two change-makers who ingrained the passion for social responsibility in me from my earliest years. As a family, we have been involved in community transformation intiatatives for as long as I can remember. Now living in Asia, I continue our tradition, as I work with organizations to help them integrate their CSR objectves and activities with their bottom line.
